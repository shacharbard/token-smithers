<objective>
Ship Tier 1 of the tiered cache coherence system: a universal content-hash manifest that works with zero external dependencies on 100% of users. Every file-scoped cached tool response records the paths it touched plus their SHA256 hashes; on lookup, any file change → invalidation. This is the table-stakes baseline for GA2 and must land before Tier 2 (wave 5/6) can consume it.
</objective>
